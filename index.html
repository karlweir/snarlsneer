Thought's On Building My Own Website

It's easy to feel panicked by a task as large as building your own website... on your own... just you. Most print houses will give a number different people different jobs. A person in charge of setting the typeface, a person in charge of layout, a person in charge of coloring and so on. But building a website on your own is equivilent to doing all of these things as a one man show. You decide the correct fonts to use that are appropriate for the purpose. You decide whether your website will be responsive for mobile layouts. You decide the entire colour-scheme of your website. And all of that can feel pretty daunting. I know I felt daunted.


But as long as you are patient and determined it becomes a really rewarding experience. There isn't a better feeling than being free to conjure any idea you like for a website and knowing you have the technical chops to pull it off.
